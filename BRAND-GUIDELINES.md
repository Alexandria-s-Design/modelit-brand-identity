# ModelIt K-12 Brand Guidelines

**Version 1.0 | December 2025**

## Brand Overview

### Mission
Empowering K-12 students with computational modeling and systems thinking skills through accessible, engaging, and scientifically rigorous educational experiences.

### Vision
Every middle school student gains the systems thinking skills needed to understand complex biological, ecological, and scientific phenomena through hands-on computational modeling.

### Brand Personality
- **Scientific**: Rooted in real research and biological accuracy
- **Accessible**: Complex concepts made understandable for grades 5-8
- **Engaging**: Interactive, visual, and hands-on learning experiences
- **Empowering**: Students as active scientists, not passive learners
- **Progressive**: Forward-thinking educational technology

### Target Audiences

**Primary**: Middle school science teachers (grades 5-8)
- 80% female, ages 28-55
- Teaching biology, life science, physical science
- Seeking NGSS-aligned resources
- Value student engagement and ease of use

## Color System

### Primary Colors

**Primary Dark Blue** - `#1F4E79`
- RGB: 31, 78, 121 | CMYK: 74, 36, 0, 53
- **Use**: Primary headings, navigation, professional materials
- **Accessibility**: WCAG AAA on white (9.23:1 contrast)

**Primary Light Blue** - `#0078D7`
- RGB: 0, 120, 215 | CMYK: 100, 44, 0, 16
- **Use**: CTAs, interactive elements, links, accents
- **Accessibility**: WCAG AA on white (4.53:1 contrast)

### Secondary Colors

**Secondary Navy** - `#2B2B40`
- RGB: 43, 43, 64 | CMYK: 33, 33, 0, 75
- **Use**: Body text, secondary headings, borders
- **Accessibility**: WCAG AAA on white (12.47:1 contrast)

**Background Light** - `#F2F6FA`
- RGB: 242, 246, 250 | CMYK: 3, 2, 0, 2
- **Use**: Page backgrounds, section dividers, card backgrounds

### Accent Colors

**Accent Teal** - `#009999`
- RGB: 0, 153, 153 | CMYK: 100, 0, 0, 40
- **Use**: Science themes, molecular diagrams, biology topics
- **Accessibility**: WCAG AA on white (4.55:1 contrast)

**Accent Gold** - `#FFC857`
- RGB: 255, 200, 87 | CMYK: 0, 22, 66, 0
- **Use**: Highlights, achievements, important callouts
- **Requires dark text**: Navy (#2B2B40) achieves AAA (7.02:1 contrast)

## Logo Guidelines

### Logo Variations
- **Primary Logo**: Full color horizontal (200px min width)
- **Stacked Logo**: Icon + wordmark vertical (150px min width)
- **Icon Only**: For social media, favicons (64×64px min)
- **Monochrome**: Navy, Dark Blue, or White versions

### Clear Space
- Maintain clear space equal to height of "M" in ModelIt on all sides

### Logo Don'ts
- Don't stretch, skew, or distort
- Don't rotate or apply effects
- Don't place on busy backgrounds without contrast treatment

## Brand Voice

### Voice Attributes
- **Educational** (not academic): Clear, instructive, scaffolded
- **Approachable** (not casual): Warm, encouraging, supportive
- **Scientific** (not stuffy): Evidence-based, research-backed
- **Empowering** (not prescriptive): Focuses on teacher and student agency
- **Progressive** (not trendy): Forward-looking but grounded

### Key Messages
1. **Standards-Aligned**: "100% NGSS-aligned, saving you planning time"
2. **Accessible Technology**: "No coding required - intuitive drag-and-drop"
3. **Real Science**: "The same tools researchers use, adapted for middle school"
4. **Systems Thinking**: "Develop critical thinking beyond biology"
5. **Proven Engagement**: "92% of teachers report increased student engagement"
